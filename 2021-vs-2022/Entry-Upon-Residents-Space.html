32. ENTRY  UPON RESIDENT'S SPACE. Management shall have a right of entry upon resident's space and mobilehome in keeping with Civil Code Section 798.26, as it may be revised from time to time. Management may not enter the space in a manner or at a time that would interfere with resident's quiet enjoyment thereof.
