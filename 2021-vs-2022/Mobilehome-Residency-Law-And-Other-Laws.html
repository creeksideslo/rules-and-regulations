26. MOBILEHOME RESIDENCY LAW AND OTHER LAWS. In addition to the information herein, resident's attention is directed to the MRL, which is incorporated by reference as though fully set forth herein and a copy of which will be provided to resident annually. A number of important provisions affecting all residents and Creekside are included in the MRL. Among the more important provisions are those concerning tenancy, mobilehome maintenance, transferring mobilehomes, public nuisance and rules and regulations.
