3. REGISTERED OWNERS AND GUESTS.  At all times, at least one (1) person occupying each mobilehome in Creekside on a full-time basis must be the "registered owner" of the mobilehome. The residents of the mobilehome must be the owner, the relatives of the owner, or must reside with the owner as provided herein, in the Lease Agreement, and in the MRL.
